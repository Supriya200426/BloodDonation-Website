We're on a Mission!

Our Mission is to bring volunteers and organisations together to share time,
skills and passion to promote India social development.


People's Organisation delivers impact in these areas-Blood Donation, Organ Donation, Education, Tribal Welfare, Youth Welfare, Livelihood & Economic Development, Poverty Alleviation, Gender Equality, Human Rights. we achieve our mission by working closely with the volunteers at one end and with government & other organisations that work on these impact areas at the other end.

Our Achievements:
  • 10,000+ Donors:Our ever-growing community of over 10,000 registered donors is a testament to the power of collective goodwill.
  • 400 Bypass Surgeries:We have successfully supported 400 bypass surgeries at the Government General Hospital in Guntur, providing critical blood supplies and saving countless lives.
  • Heart Transplants:Our efforts have facilitated four heart transplant surgeries at government hospitals, giving patients a new lease on life.
  • Emergency Response:We have played a crucial role in numerous surgeries and emergency situations, including road accident cases, by ensuring timely blood donations.
