A STEP TO SAVE A LIFE


Why Donate Blood?

Your Blood Can Save Lives

  • Life-Saving Impact: Each blood donation can save up to three lives, providing critical support in emergencies and medical procedures.
  • Community Support:By donating blood, you become a hero in your community, helping those in desperate need.
  • Health Benefits:Regular blood donation has been linked to a reduced risk of heart disease and cancer, and it contributes to overall cardiovascular health.
  • Free Health Check-Up:Each donation includes a health screening, offering insights into your health status.

Eligibility Rules:

  • Age between 18 and 60 years
  • Haemoglobin - not less than 12.5 g/Dl
  • Pulse - between 50 and 100/minute with no irregularities
  • Temperature - Normal (oral temperature not exceeding 37.50C)
  • weight - not less than 45 Kg
  • Women should not be pregnant or breast feeding her child
  • Past 24 hours - taken alcoholic beverages
